Steven Universe

Who is Steven Universe?

Steven Quartz Universe is the titular main protagonist of the Steven Universe franchise.

Fun Facts

  • Steven's name and appearance are based on Rebecca Sugar's brother, Steven Sugar.
  • When viewed from the back, the bumps of Steven's hair form a rose shape
  • Since Steven's biological age can be controlled by his state of mind, he possibly could live forever.
